I'm Not Trying to Seduce You

Posted: 2024-06-27

I write. And I do write for people. And for people I write about weird shit I think. Because it’s fun. For people, I write about weird things I feel. After all, everyone does. I just have no shame about it. And I’ll write so everybody knows. I’m still human. And I’ll write about my human nature. I’m still to overcome it. But in the meanwhile, I’ll write about the weird way it goes.

I’m not trying to seduce you.
